ABOUT US

The Pub opened its doors in October 2012. In just two short years, we were able to double in size and add the kitchen we have always wanted! We offer an authentic and memorable experience for those sitting down to dine, or others enjoying the bar and nightlife. We promise one thing: there will be a culinary culture to The Pub both from a food and beverage perspective. And we want you — as a guest — to be part of it!

Atmosphere. That is the mainstay of a great pub. All things being equal, atmosphere is really what makes The Pub's stand head and heels over our competition. .We treat people right, and that's what makes new guests feel welcome, and regulars feel right at home.
